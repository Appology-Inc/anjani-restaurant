Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Anjani Delivery app, we collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, phone number, and delivery addresses. For Delivery Partners, we also collect precise location data (GPS) to enable live order tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our food delivery services, process your transactions, send you technical notices and support messages, and communicate with you about your orders.

3. Location Data (For Delivery Partners)

If you are a Delivery Partner, we collect precise location data from your device while the app is active to provide live tracking for customers. This is essential for the core functionality of the delivery service.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@anjanikindergarten.com.
